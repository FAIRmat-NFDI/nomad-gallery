    Welcome to the #nomad-gallery¶

    Welcome to #nomad-gallery — a space to showcase innovative features, uploads, and use cases created with the NOMAD platform. Whether you want to share your latest work or explore what’s possible, this is the place to engage, discover, and inspire.

    Submitter: Joseph Rudzinski

    Description: The Alexandria database is being ingested into NOMAD! The result is tens of millions of NOMAD calculations entries that can be efficiently queried and explored via NOMAD's extensive search and filtering functionalities.
